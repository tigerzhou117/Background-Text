App Introduction

Put text BEHIND your photo subjects with just one tap! Background Text uses advanced technology to automatically detect and place text behind the main subjects in your photos.

Key Features:

  • Smart subject detection
  • Automatic text behind subject placement
  • Multiple text layers management
  • Rich font collection
  • Text customization
  • One-tap Background filters
  • One-Tap Background Swap/Color Change
  • Unlimited undo/redo
  • High-quality export
  • Local image processing, protect your privacy

Perfect for:

  • Creating depth effect photos
  • Making creative social posts
  • Designing unique wallpapers
  • Professional photography
  • Marketing materials
  • Social media content
  • Artistic photo editing
  • Change the background color of the ID photo
Notice: The first time running the app, network permission is required to download fonts.
